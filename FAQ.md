- How many devices can I add to a user?
	- One user can support up to 128 GPS modules. 
- What is the receiver to GPS module working radius?
	- The receiver can locate the GPS module within a 5 mile radius of the receiver, feel free to lose your TUO anywhere on site!
- Is the GPS module durable?
	- Yes! with our 3D printed case, the module can withstand freezing temperatures and drops up to 10 feet.
- How do I remove a GPS module?
	- Once your module is located in the home page, click into it to view the specific module information. Scroll down to the bottom of the page and click "Remove from User".
